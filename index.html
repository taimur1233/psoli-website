Who we are زموږ په اړه

We came from different valleys. We found each other here.

The Pashtuns Society of Long Island is a family. We come from different cities and valleys, and we built a home together here on Long Island.

PSOLI exists so our children grow up knowing where they come from — the poetry, the food, the music, and the manners — and so we can stand beside one another as neighbors when it matters most.

What we do زموږ کارونه

Ways we keep our culture close.

Cultural gatherings

Eid celebrations, picnics, poetry and music that bring families together all year.

Language & heritage

Helping the next generation hold on to Pashto and the stories that come with it.

Community support

Showing up for one another — new arrivals, weddings, hard times, everything between.

Preserving our stories

Collecting photos, memories, and history so our journey here is never forgotten.
