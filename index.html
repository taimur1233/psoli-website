Who we are زموږ په اړه

Preserving heritage. Caring for community.

The Pashtuns Society of Long Island is a nonprofit cultural and community organization — nonpolitical and nonreligious — built by families who came from different valleys and made a home together here.

We keep our language, hospitality, and traditions alive, and we look after one another: food for families in need, mentoring for our youth, and a helping hand through life's hardest days.

Our programs زموږ کارونه

Ways we show up for our community.

Food pantry

Groceries and warm meals for families facing hard times — no questions, just help.

Youth mentoring

Tutoring, guidance, and leadership for the next generation — rooted in Pashto and pride.

Housing & settling help

Guidance for new arrivals — finding housing, paperwork, and getting on their feet.

Culture & gatherings

Eid, picnics, poetry and music that keep our heritage alive for every generation.
