What we do

Four simple ways we keep our community close and our culture alive.

Cultural gatherings

Eid celebrations, summer picnics, poetry evenings, music and food — the moments that bring families together across the year and remind us of home.

Language & heritage

Helping our children hold on to Pashto — its poetry, proverbs, and stories — so our identity carries into the next generation, here on Long Island.

Community support

Standing beside one another — welcoming new arrivals, celebrating weddings and births, and being there for families through life's harder days.

Preserving our stories

Collecting photos, memories, and the history of our families' journey so that the story of Pashtuns on Long Island is never forgotten.
